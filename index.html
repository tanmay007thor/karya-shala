'KARYASHALA' is a noble endeavor of Government of India by Science and Engineering Board (SERB) under Accelerate Vigyan Scheme to improve research productivity of promising PG and PhD students from universities and colleges through high-end workshops on specific themes. This program aims to provide opportunities to acquire specialized research skills. These workshops will primarily be facilitated at organizations / institutions / laboratories of national importance. PDEU Gandhinagar has decided to be a part of this unique initiative and share our Institutional resources with the society towards higher goals of national importance. Thus, the current workshop has come into being. This event is sponsored by SERB.

Sponsored By:


Joint IEEE Chapter of Solid-State Circuits and Electron Devices (SSC & ED) Societies, Gujarat Section

About the Workshop

The current high-end workshop can be highly beneficial to students and researchers working in the area of AI/ML, Hardware Implementation, VLSI Architectures and Systems, MATLAB/Python, HDL ‚Äì Verilog, FPGA. The workshop aims to provide opportunities to acquire specialized research skills and hands-on-training.

Basics of AI/ML approaches and their implementation.

Introduction to VLSI design and methodologies.

VLSI architectures and hardware for AI/ML algorithms.

Hands-on training using CAD tools for application oriented VLSI system designs and AI/ML projects.

Speakers

The course faculty includes resource persons from IITs, NITs, IIITs, reputed institutes and industries.

Who Can Attend

Workshop is open to postgraduate students/research scholars from all Institutes and universities. Preference will be given to thestudents having interest towards AI/ML, VLSI Architectures, Embedded Systems, Hardware Implementation, MATLAB/Python, HDL - Verilog, FPGA, SPICE.

Total number of seats is limited to 25. The participants will be selected based on their academic/research credentials and first-come-first-serve basis. The selected applicants will be provided free accommodation and fooding at PDEU. Also, the traveling expenses (3 AC) will be reimbursed .

About Gandhinagar

With the tag of the cleanest city in Asia, Gandhinagar is the administrative capital of the state of Gujarat, located on the western bank of Sabarmati river. Named after the father of Nation, Mahatma Gandhi, Gandhinagar is the second planned city of India, after Chandigarh. The city is divided into thirty organised sectors, each of them is pedestrian friendly and self-contained. It's more of a people-oriented city, with all the parks, monuments, gardens and grand civic buildings. During Rainy season, i.e., when the workshop is scheduled, it is pleasant climate with temperature ranging between 25 to 35 ¬∞C. It is advisable to bring umbrella with you.
